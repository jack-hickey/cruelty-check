Cruelty Check About

I created Cruelty Check to help people who want to live vegan or eco-conscious lifestyles more easily find products that align with their values. The project is community-driven, with submissions coming directly from people like you and me. Each submission is carefully reviewed and moderated before being shared with everyone.

One of the biggest challenges of living vegan is understanding the business practices of the companies we support. Cruelty Check is designed to simplify that process. It highlights key brand information, such as animal testing policies and cruelty-free status, right when you need it. The platform also considers parent companies, so if your favorite brand is owned (or indirectly owned) by a company that tests on animals, you'll be informed.

Feedback is always welcome, and you'll find several ways to share it. You can use the feedback button at the top of the page to report an issue, suggest an improvement, or simply say thanks! You can also flag a product if you believe some information may be incorrect. And of course, new product submissions are encouraged, once they're reviewed and approved, they'll appear on the site for everyone to use.

Privacy is important, and Cruelty Check's aim is simply to provide an easy access route into the vegan world, so why would it use cookies? It doesn't! Cruelty Check doesn't use cookies or trackers in any capacity, and doesn't collect any personal data when you visit the site.

I use Cloudflare to keep the site nice and fast for you (and more importantly, secure). This means some basic, non-personal information may be processed temporarily for security but it is never stored and I don't use it in any shape or form.

Please note that while submissions are provided in good faith by the amazing community and are reviewed and moderated, the information may not always be 100% accurate. For complete certainty, you are encouraged to check official sources.
